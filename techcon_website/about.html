Our History

Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has grown from a small local event into one of the most influential global technology conferences. Over the years, it has provided a platform for innovators, startups, and established companies to share ideas and showcase breakthroughs.

Our Mission

TechCon is committed to driving innovation, fostering collaboration, and inspiring the next generation of technology leaders. Our mission is to create a space where diverse voices in the tech community can come together to solve global challenges.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted a variety of remarkable speakers who have left a lasting impact on the industry and inspired future innovators.

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Known as the first computer programmer, Dr. Lovelace’s vision of computing extended far beyond her time. She delivered the opening keynote at TechCon 2015, inspiring attendees with her pioneering ideas.

Alan Turing

A key figure in computer science, Turing’s contributions to artificial intelligence continue to influence modern research. His TechCon 2017 address highlighted the ethical implications of machine learning.

Grace Hopper

Admiral Grace Hopper, a pioneer of programming languages, shared her insights on the future of software at TechCon 2019. Her leadership and innovation paved the way for generations of developers.
